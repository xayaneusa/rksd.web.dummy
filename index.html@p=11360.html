Won Overall, Champion Trophy of Ratnawali organized by Kurukshetra University, Kurukshetra (25th to 28th October, 2024). 

Overall Trophy winner of Zonal Youth Festival organized during 21-23, October 2024

Covocation Organized on 19.10.2024.  Sh. Vikas Gupta, IAS presided over the function as Chief Guest.

Alumni Meet Organized on 19.10.2024.  Sh. Ashwani Shorewala, President, Governing body and RVS presided over the function as Chief Guest.

Teacher Day celebrated on its Pre-Eve on 04.09.2024. Prof. Somnath Sachdeva, Vice-chancellor, Kurukshetra University, Kurukshetra presided over as Chief Guest. 

The Department of Economics and Research Committee of the College organized an online Multidisciplinary National Workshop on ‘Understanding Research Ethics, Plagiarism, and Research Metrics’ on April 27, 2024. The workshop was inaugurated by College Principal Dr. Sanjay Goyal through a lamp lighting ceremony.

The 70th Annual Sports Meet was organized on February 23, 2024, at RKSD College, Kaithal. Sh. Saket Mangal, Advocate , President RVS & Governing Body, RKSD College, Katihal presided over the function as Chief Guest.

The Department of Physical Education and Sports Science, RKSD Kaithal in association with the Sports Wing of the Art of Living Foundation, organized a one-day workshop on the importance of mediation and the impact of steriods. Sh. Mukesh Garg, Member Haryana Electricity Development Board, was the Chief guest.  (04.02.2024)

Smt. Mamta Sodha, Assistant Commissioner of Police, Panchkula presided over  the Annual Talent Search Competition as  Chief Guest at RKSD College, Kaithal on 16th September 2023

Professor Ramesh chander Bhardwaj, Vice-Chancellor, Maharshi Valmiki Sanskrit University, Mundri, Kaithal presided over as Chief Guest on the occasion of Teachers’ Day Function organized in the college on 5th September, 2023.

 

One-day PDP-cum National workshop on Dynamics of Educational Resources and Reading Culture” organized  by RKSD College, Kaithal in collaboration with Harynan Library Association on August 12, 2023.  Prof. Sanjeev Sharma, Registrar, Kurukshetra University Kurukshetra presided over as Chief Guest.   

62nd R. K. S. D. Annual Athletic Meet (03.03.2023)- Prize Distribution

43rd Inter-Zonal Youth Festival-2022 organized  by RKSD (PG) College, Kaithal and sponsored by DYCA, Kurukshetra University, Kurukshetra (16-18 December, 2022)

Sh. Tribhuvan Dahiya Hon’ble Judge Punjab and Haryana High court, Chandigarh visited RKSD (PG) college, Kaithal as a Chief Guest in Convocation-2022 held on 11.12.2022

Alumni Meet-2022 Organized on 11.12.2022

The college performed grand performance in the state level cultural program ‘Ratnavali’  during 28th-31st OCtober 2022  held the occasion of Haryana Day at Kurukshetra University.

RKSD (PG) college has won the Over All Trophy of the 45th Zonal Youth Festival (2022) at the Janata College during 15th to 17th October 2022) 

Seminar on “The Saraswati River: The Cradle of Indian Civilization” organized by Haryana Saraswati Heritage Development Board (HSHDB) & Centre of Excellence for research on Saraswati River (CERSR) , Kuruksehtra University Kurukshetra in collaboration with RKSD (PG) College, Kaithal on 11.10.2022.  H.E. Dr. Roger Gopaul, High Commissioner , High Commission of the Republic of Trinidad and Tobacco was the Chief Guest of the Seminar

Awareness programme on cyber Crime was organized on 06.10.2022 by Police Administration, Kaithal in collaboration with Department of Political Science, RKSD(PG) College, Kaithal

Two-Day Multidisciplinary National Seminar organized during June 11-12, 2022 on “Vision of Independent India: An Evaluation of 75 years of Experience” sponsored by Director Higher Education, Haryana, Panchkula 

Gian Chand Gupta, Speaker of  Haryana Legislative Assembly inaugurated the newly constructed “Maharaja Aggarsain Bhawan” in RKSD (PG) College, Kaithal on 09.10.2021

75rd Independence Day celebrated as ‘Amrit Mahotsav of Independence’ in College premises and Sh. Saket Mangal. President, Governing Body, RKSD(PG) College, Kaithal, hosted the National Flag on the occasion.

Plantation campaign on the occasion of International Olympic Sports Day (24th June 2021

An event ‘Excerpts from the Biographies of Great Personalities’ organized by Library Committee on 26.02.2021

Extension Lecture delivered by Prof. Mohinder Singh, Former Professor & Chairman, Department of Public Administration, Kurukshetra University, Kurukshetra on 09.02.2021 in PG Seminar Room at 11:00 AM (organized by Political Science Department, RKSD (PG) College, Kaithal)

Student’s Workshop on ‘Economic Survey and Union Budget’ organized by Economics Association of Department of Economics on February 8, 2021.

Talent Show (Pratibha Khoj) Organized on 14.01.2021 in R.K.S.D (PG) College, Kaithal
