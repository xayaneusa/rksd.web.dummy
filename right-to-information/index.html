Right to Information

About the Act

Right to Information Act 2005 mandates timely response to citizen requests for government information. It is an initiative taken by Department of Personnel and Training, Ministry of Personnel, Public Grievances and Pensions to provide a– RTI Portal Gateway to the citizens for quick search of information on the details of first Appellate Authorities,PIOs etc. amongst others, besides access to RTI related information / disclosures published on the web by various Public Authorities under the government of India as well as the State Governments.

Objectives

The basic object of the Right to Information Act is to empower the citizens,promote transparency and accountability in the working of the Government,contain corruption, and make our democracy work for the people in real sense.It goes without saying that an informed citizen is better equipped to keep necessary vigil on the instruments of governance and make the government more accountable to the governed.The Act is a big step towards making the citizens informed about the activities of the Government.

Information under Section 4 of Right to information Act,2005
  1. The Particulars of its Organization, functions and duties.
    R.K.S.D. College, Kaithal is an affiliated college of Kurukshetra University, Kurukshetra and Accredited with A Grade by the NAAC during the Session 2016-17.The College is recognized under section 2(f) and 12(b) of UGC. This college is established by Rashtriya Vidya Samiti. RVS (Regd.) Kaithal Registered on 18-01-1954 vide registration No. 78 of 1953-54 under Societies Registration Act XXI of 1860 and afterwards re-registered under HRRS Act, 2012 under Registration No. HR-005-2016-01367 dated 28-12-2016. Now; the total members of the society are 3714 in number. The college imparts education leading to the degrees of B.A., B.SC. (Medical, Non-Medical, Electronics, computer science), B.COM, BBA, BCA, MA (English, Hindi, Political Science) and M.SC Mathematics.
  2. The Powers and Duties of the officers and Employees. Principal is the Academic Officer of the college. He is responsible for appropriate administration and organization of teaching and extra-curricular activities in the college. The teaching faculty members apart from their duties of teaching and guiding the students at different levels engaged in administrative and Co-curricular duties for the smooth running of the institution.

  3. The procedure followed in the decision making process, including channels of supervision and accountability.In this institution all the decisions are taken as per the rules and regulations of Kurukshetra University, Kurukshetra and Director General Higher Education Panchkula. The college functions under the general supervision and control by the different Committees constituted by the Principal for Academic session.

  4. The norms set by it for the discharge of its functions. Norms for various academic activities of the college are set by the competent authority such as Executive and Academic Council of the affiliating University and by the Advisory council and Governing Body of the college in addition to the norms fixed by the State Govt.

  5. The act, statues and regulations held by it or under its control or used by its employees for discharging its functions.
    This college is affiliated from Kurukshetra University, Kurukshetra so all the act statutes and calendar of Kurukshetra University Kurukshetra(Linked below) are applicable to this college.

Kaithal. Out of 21 Collegiums Four office bearers of the Governing Body of the College are elected. The President of the Governing Body nominates eleven members out of 3714 Members of the Society. One member nominated by the Vice-Chancellor of the Affiliated University and One nominee nominated by Director General Higher Education Haryana, Two Representatives from the Teaching faculty and one Representative from the Non-teaching staff. Principal of the College is the Member secretary of the Governing Body to record the minutes of the meetings of the Governing Body.The details of Members and office bearers are given on college website under RVS Link and in Information brochure.
